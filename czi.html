A little about me

I believe in products that work and provide real benefit for people. I believe in failing fast and learning quickly. I believe that technology and design aren’t inherently evil, but that we have to work to make sure that we use them for good. I also believe that CZI shares my beliefs, and could be a place where I feel that I have a real impact on the world.


As a person and designer who is mixed race, has moved over 15 times all over the globe, and as a result has a strong perspective on diversity and inclusivity, I appreciate CZI’s commitment to hiring marginalized groups, diverse applicants, and fresh perspectives. I like to think that I would be a good fit for your community, and can’t wait to to challenge and be challenged every day.

What I've Got

5 years as a product designer, ideally with interaction and visual design experience working on top of complex, ranked data.

I have over five years of experience working with product managers, engineers, researchers, and experts in fields to deliver valuable, clear experiences.


My most recent experience as a UX designer at Cisco encompasses taking complex, enterprise class data, and distilling it into understandable, actionable information for a highly technical user. See my case study about DNA Center for an example.

Demonstrated experience with end-to-end product design, including clear and effective interactions, to accomplish product or business goals.

Both in my experience working in big tech as well as in design agencies, I’ve have the opportunity to work on small teams where I have the ability to work on products from end to end. I enjoy being able to be involved with product from start to finish, and appreciate that design can address needs beyond visual aesthetics.


See an example of my end-to-end product design process in my case study for Culture Club

Comfort integrating qualitative and quantitative data to pragmatically learn and iterate towards high-impact product ideas. Experience with effective A/B testing is a plus.

As a designer with a Masters Degree in HCI, I have a strong understanding of research, and pride myself in being a data driven designer. While I have experience working with researchers, I currently work on a design team where we conduct all of our own design research, both generative and evaluative. See an example of a designed research read out document that I created here.

Excellent communication skills and ability to explain rationale for product design recommendations.

UX and design thinking is relatively new at Cisco. As a result, I’ve had to do plenty of evangelizing within my own team and at a company culture level. Being able to explain product design recommendations and rationale to stakeholders is an integral part of showing the value of design, and will always be a part of my design practice. Watch me talk about some of my work and process here.

Demonstrated mastery of typography, grid systems, color theory, and other aspects of visual design with strong attention to detail

Demonstrated visual design skill, especially in the construction of visual hierarchies, and attention to detail on typography, spacing, and color
